The Evolution of Human-made Fires Over Time

The majority of wildfires in the United States are caused by humans, accounting for 84% of all wildfires and 44% of the total area burned.
The chart shows the number and size of wildfires ignited by humans on U.S. Forest Service land from 2000 to 2018.
